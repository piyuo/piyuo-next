Privacy Policy for Piyuo Counter

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Piyuo Counter! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our software application ("App"). Our commitment is to your privacy. This App is designed to function without collecting or processing any of your personal data.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Piyuo Counter software application ("App") for mobile devices and computers, available across various platforms and application stores.

Who We Are

The Piyuo Counter app is provided by Piyuo ("we," "us," or "our"). Our website is https://piyuo.com. If you have questions about this policy, you can contact us at service@piyuo.com.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or usage data from you through the Piyuo Counter app.

  • No Personal Data: We do not ask for, access, or track any information that could identify you, such as your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or contacts.
  • No Usage Data: The app does not record how you use it. All counter data you create is stored locally on your device only and is not accessible to us.
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not integrate any third-party services for analytics (like Firebase Analytics), advertising (like AdMob), cloud storage, or any other purpose that would involve sharing data with external parties. The app operates entirely offline in terms of data handling.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use your information for any purpose.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose any personal information because we do not collect any. Your data (the counts you track) stays on your device.

Data Security

Any data generated by using the Piyuo Counter app (such as your counts) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data. While we build our app with standard security practices, the security of the data stored on your device depends on the security measures you take for your device itself.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the US and similar regulations like GDPR regarding children's data. As we collect no data, we inherently do not collect data from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in some EU countries).

Your Rights (GDPR and Other Laws)

Privacy laws like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in Europe and various US state laws grant individuals rights over their personal data (like access, correction, deletion).

Because the Piyuo Counter app does not collect, store, or process any of your personal data, these rights are generally not applicable in the context of our App, as there is no data held by us for you to access, correct, or delete. Any data related to the app resides solely on your device, under your control.
